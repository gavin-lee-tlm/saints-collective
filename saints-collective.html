Church Plant

West Palm Beach, Florida

Our flagship church plant — governed directly by The Saints Collective — is a Spirit-filled sending church for South Florida and beyond.

What We're Building

A church that prays, gathers,
and goes

A gathering place where the Holy Spirit moves, disciples are formed, and believers are commissioned and sent. Every program we run is oriented toward the Great Commission.

Beach Evangelism

Regular outreach teams taking the Gospel to South Florida beaches, boardwalks, and public spaces.

Shuttle Ministry

A dedicated shuttle picks up and drops off community members — removing every barrier to belonging.

The Saints Café

A coffee and sandwich shop operating solely for the benefit of the church — a gathering place and ministry revenue stream.

Mission Merch

All merchandise sold exclusively funds the church plant's mission and community programs.

South Florida Events

Discipleship nights, community gatherings, and missions mobilization events across the region.

Governance

Nonprofit-governed,
mission-driven

The West Palm Beach church is a direct program of The Saints Collective 501(c)(3) — providing legal structure, financial accountability, and strategic direction from day one.

What this means: The Saints Collective board governs the church plant. All donations are tax-deductible under our 501(c)(3). As the congregation grows and matures, it can be incorporated and commissioned as its own independent church — a natural sending moment.

What We Do

Five pillars of Kingdom work

Every initiative is rooted in the Great Commission and the Great Commandment — love God, love people, make disciples, advance justice.

01

Church Planting

Planting and governing Spirit-filled churches, beginning in West Palm Beach — a sending church oriented toward the Great Commission and the nations.

02

International Missions

Partnering with YWAM Seville's farm base in Spain — providing financial support and producing a documentary on what God is doing through this global outpost.

03

Justice & Reform

Supporting organizations on the frontlines of human trafficking rehabilitation, civic leadership development, and advocacy for the vulnerable in our communities.

04

Grants & Partnerships

Funding vetted Christian nonprofits and charitable initiatives aligned with our mission. The body is stronger together — strategic generosity multiplies Kingdom impact.

05

Movement & Media

Connecting Christian creators, artists, pastors, and leaders for unified Kingdom missions — commissioning worship music, documentaries, and faith-based platforms.

Connected to The Saints Club

10% of all Saints Club membership and merchandise proceeds flow directly into The Saints Collective — a built-in generosity model that scales with the mission.
